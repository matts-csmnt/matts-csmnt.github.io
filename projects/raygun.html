[C/C++] Raygun - Software Pathtracer


Following Pete Shirley's books on Raytracing, along with other great resources, I've been creating a small software pathtracing library.
The project currently supports:

  • Materials: Lambertian, Metallics, Dielectrics, Fog Volumes, Constant Texture
  • Textures: Image Based, Generated (Noise, Algorithmic patterning)
  • Diffuse Lighting
  • Bounding Volume Hierarchy Acceleration (w/ Axis Aligned Bounding Boxes)

In the future I aim to have meshes and CUDA support to offload the work to the GPU.
