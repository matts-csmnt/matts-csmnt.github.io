[C++] Screen Space Ambient Occlusion


For this project I undertook a ‘mini-research’ project into implementing and analysing the performance of SSAO for games.

This included looking at methods of creating the occlusion buffer, de-noising and smoothing the results and optimisations for getting good visual results at fast speeds. The framework I used implemented a deferred pipeline. Over the course of the project I researched and/or implemented:

  • Screen Space Ambient Occlusion
  • Gaussian Blurs (traditional & optimised)
  • Kawase Blur
  • Down & Upsampling Image Buffers

Combining these techniques, I found that a satisfactory effect could be achieved in under 1ms in a game running at 60fps.
